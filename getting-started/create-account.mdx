Creating a Clamly account takes less than a minute and requires no credit card. Sign up with Google for a one-click setup, or register manually with your name, email, and a password.

## Sign up for Clamly

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to clamly.app">
    Open [clamly.app](https://clamly.app) in your browser. On the homepage, click the **Start for free** button or the **Create your account** link to open the registration page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your sign-up method">
    Clamly offers two ways to register:

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Continue with Google">
        Click **Continue with Google**, pick your Google account, and approve the requested permissions. Clamly pulls in your name and email automatically, so there's no form to fill out.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Sign up with email">
        Fill in the registration form:

        | Field | Requirement |
        | --- | --- |
        | **Name** | Your first and last name |
        | **Email** | A valid email address you have access to |
        | **Password** | At least 8 characters |

        Then click **Create account**.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy">
    Before your account is created, you must agree to Clamly's [Terms of Service](https://clamly.app/terms) and [Privacy Policy](https://clamly.app/privacy). Check the agreement box to confirm you have read and accepted both documents.

    <Warning>
      You must be at least **13 years old** to create a Clamly account. If you are under 13, you are not permitted to register.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access your dashboard">
    Once registration is complete, Clamly takes you straight to your personal dashboard. From here you can start creating quizzes, writing notes, building flashcard decks, and more, with no extra setup.

    <Tip>
      Bookmark your dashboard URL so you can return quickly from any device. Clamly works in any modern browser, no app install needed.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Things to know before you sign up

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="One account per person">
    You can only hold one Clamly account. The Terms of Service do not permit you to create multiple accounts for yourself.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Age requirement">
    Clamly requires all users to be at least 13 years old. By completing registration, you confirm that you meet this age requirement.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="No credit card needed">
    Signing up for the free plan requires no payment information. You only need billing details if you choose to upgrade to Starter or Pro.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Need help?">
    If you run into any issues during registration, reach out to the Clamly support team at [support@clamly.app](mailto:support@clamly.app).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
