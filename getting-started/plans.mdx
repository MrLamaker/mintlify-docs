Clamly offers three plans so you can choose the level of AI power and features that fits the way you study. Every plan includes the full set of core study tools — the difference lies in how many AI sessions you get and access to advanced extras. No credit card is required to get started on the free plan, and you can upgrade or cancel at any time.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need a credit card to start?">
    No. The Free plan requires no payment information. You only need to add billing details when you choose to upgrade to Starter or Pro.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I upgrade my plan?">
    Go to the **Billing** page inside your Clamly dashboard and select the plan you want. Payment is processed securely through **Stripe** — you'll be prompted to enter your card details before the upgrade takes effect.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I manage or cancel my subscription?">
    You can manage your subscription from the **Billing** page in Clamly or directly through the **Stripe customer portal**, where you can update payment methods, view invoices, and cancel at any time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Will I lose my data if I downgrade or cancel?">
    Your notes, flashcards, and other content remain in your account if you downgrade. Your AI session limit will return to the Free tier allocation. Contact [support@clamly.app](mailto:support@clamly.app) if you have concerns about your data.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are there any long-term contracts?">
    No. All paid plans are billed monthly with no long-term commitment. You can cancel at any time from the Billing page or Stripe customer portal, and you won't be charged again after the current billing period ends.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What payment methods are accepted?">
    Clamly uses Stripe to process payments, which supports all major credit and debit cards as well as other payment methods available in your region.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  Not sure which plan to pick? Start on Free — you can upgrade at any moment from your Billing page once you know how much you rely on AI features.
</Tip>
