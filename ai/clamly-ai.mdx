Clamly AI takes the heavy lifting out of exam prep. Drop in your lecture notes, a PDF, or a Word document and Clamly AI reads through everything, then hands you back a ready-to-use quiz and a concise summary — no formatting, no copy-pasting, no extra steps required.

## Supported File Types

You can feed Clamly AI content in any of the following formats:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="PDF" icon="file-pdf">
    Upload scanned readings, lecture slides exported as PDF, or any PDF document from your course.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Word Document" icon="file-word">
    Upload `.docx` files — ideal for typed notes, essays, or downloaded study guides.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Plain Text" icon="file-lines">
    Upload `.txt` files for lightweight, no-frills text content.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Paste Text" icon="clipboard">
    No file? No problem. Paste your notes or any block of text directly into the input field.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to Generate a Quiz from Your Material

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Clamly AI">
    From your dashboard, navigate to the **Clamly AI** section in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload or Paste Your Content">
    Click **Upload File** to select a PDF, `.docx`, or `.txt` file from your device, or paste your text directly into the text input area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Processing">
    Click **Generate** and wait a few seconds while Clamly AI reads through your material. Processing time depends on the length of the content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Your Quiz and Summary">
    Once processing is complete, you'll see your auto-generated quiz and a study-ready summary. You can start the quiz immediately or review the summary first to refresh the key ideas.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## AI Session Limits by Plan

Clamly AI usage is measured in **sessions** — each time you upload or paste content and generate output counts as one session. The number of sessions available to you depends on your plan.

| Plan | AI Sessions |
|---|---|
| **Free** | Limited sessions per period |
| **Starter** ($4.99/mo) | More sessions than Free |
| **Pro** ($9.99/mo) | Maximum sessions available |

For a full breakdown of what each plan includes, see [Plans & Pricing](/getting-started/plans).

<Tip>
  If you're running low on sessions, upgrading to Starter or Pro gives you significantly more headroom — especially useful during exam season when you're processing multiple documents.
</Tip>

## Privacy & Your Data

Clamly AI is powered by **Mistral AI**. When you upload or paste content, that content is sent to the Mistral AI API for processing.

<Note>
  Mistral AI does **not** use your content to train its models. Your study materials are processed to generate your quiz and summary, and that's it.
</Note>

If you're working with sensitive documents, avoid uploading anything beyond your own course notes and readings.

## AI Accuracy

Clamly AI is a powerful study aid, but it isn't infallible.

<Warning>
  AI-generated quizzes and summaries may contain errors, omissions, or misinterpretations. Always cross-check AI-generated content against your course materials, textbooks, or other authoritative sources before relying on it for academic work.
</Warning>

Use Clamly AI as a starting point to build your understanding — not as a replacement for your primary sources.
