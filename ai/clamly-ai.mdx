Clamly AI takes the heavy lifting out of exam prep. Drop in your lecture notes, a PDF, or a Word document and it gives you a quiz and summary ready to use.

## Supported file types

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="PDF" icon="file-type-pdf">
    Upload scanned readings, lecture slides exported as PDF, or any PDF document from your course.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Word Document" icon="file">
    Upload `.docx` files for typed notes, essays, or downloaded study guides.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Plain Text" icon="file-text">
    Upload `.txt` files for lightweight, no-frills text content.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Paste Text" icon="clipboard">
    No file? Paste your notes or any block of text directly into the input field.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to generate a quiz from your material

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Clamly AI">
    From your dashboard, navigate to the **Clamly AI** section in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload or Paste Your Content">
    Click **Upload File** to select a PDF, `.docx`, or `.txt` file from your device, or paste your text directly into the text input area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Processing">
    Click **Generate** and wait a few seconds while Clamly AI reads through your material.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Your Quiz and Summary">
    Once processing is complete, you'll see your auto-generated quiz and a study-ready summary. Start the quiz immediately or review the summary first.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you get

After processing your material, Clamly AI produces three things:

<CardGroup cols={3}>

  <Card title="Key Highlights" icon="highlight">

## AI session limits by plan

Clamly AI usage is measured in **sessions**. Each upload or paste that generates output counts as one session. The number of sessions available depends on your plan.

| Plan | AI Sessions |
|---|---|
| **Free** | Limited sessions per period |
| **Starter** ($4.99/mo) | More sessions than Free |
| **Pro** ($9.99/mo) | Maximum sessions available |

For a full breakdown of what each plan includes, see [Plans & Pricing](/getting-started/plans).

<Tip>
  If you're running low on sessions, Starter or Pro gives you more.
</Tip>

## Privacy and your data

Clamly AI is powered by **Mistral AI**. When you upload or paste content, that content is sent to the Mistral AI API for processing.

<Note>
  Mistral AI does **not** use your content to train its models. Your study materials are processed to generate your quiz and summary, and that's it.
</Note>

If you're working with sensitive documents, avoid uploading anything beyond your own course notes and readings.

## AI accuracy

<Warning>
  AI-generated quizzes and summaries may contain errors, omissions, or misinterpretations. Always cross-check against your course materials, textbooks, or other authoritative sources before relying on it for academic work.
</Warning>
