Clamly Chat is your on-demand AI study assistant. Whether you need a concept unpacked, a tricky formula explained, or a quick quiz to test yourself, Clamly Chat is ready to help — all within a conversational interface that feels like texting a study partner who actually knows the material.

## Four chat modes

Clamly Chat doesn't do one-size-fits-all. Before you start a conversation, you choose a **chat mode** that shapes how the AI responds. Pick the style that matches how you learn best right now.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Adaptive" icon="sliders">
    The AI reads your responses and adjusts its tone, depth, and pace on the fly. Great for exploratory study sessions when you're not sure exactly what you need — Clamly Chat figures it out as you go.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Tutor" icon="chalkboard-teacher">
    Breaks concepts down step by step, just like a patient teacher would. Best when you're encountering something new, struggling with a topic, or want to build understanding from the ground up.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Buddy" icon="face-smile">
    Casual, encouraging, and conversational — like a friend who actually paid attention in class. Perfect for low-pressure review sessions or when dense, formal explanations are making your eyes glaze over.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Professor" icon="graduation-cap">
    Formal, thorough, and academically precise. Choose this mode when you need rigorous, detailed answers — ideal for upper-level coursework, technical subjects, or when you want depth over brevity.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to start a chat session

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Clamly Chat">
    From your dashboard, select **Clamly Chat** in the sidebar to open the chat interface.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a Chat Mode">
    At the top of the chat panel, select your preferred mode: **Adaptive**, **Tutor**, **Buddy**, or **Professor**. You can switch modes between sessions at any time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ask Your First Question">
    Type your question or topic into the message box and click **Send**. Be as specific or as broad as you like — Clamly Chat will respond in the style of the mode you've selected.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Keep the Conversation Going">
    Follow up, ask for clarification, or go deeper on any point. The chat is conversational, so you can build on previous messages just as you would in a real tutoring session.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Asking questions and getting explanations

You can ask Clamly Chat almost anything related to your studies. Here are a few examples of how students use it:

<Accordion title="Explain a concept">
  Ask things like *"Can you explain the difference between mitosis and meiosis?"* or *"What is opportunity cost in economics?"* — Clamly Chat will walk you through it at whatever level of detail your chosen mode calls for.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Work through a problem">
  Paste in a problem or question from your coursework and ask Clamly Chat to help you reason through it. In Tutor mode especially, you'll get a step-by-step walkthrough rather than just a direct answer.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Summarise a topic">
  Ask for a quick overview of a subject area — great for jogging your memory before a lecture or reviewing a chapter before diving into the details.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Compare or contrast ideas">
  Ask things like *"What's the difference between X and Y?"* or *"When would you use approach A vs. approach B?"* to sharpen your understanding of related concepts.
</Accordion>

## Turn your chat into a quiz

At any point in a conversation, you can ask Clamly Chat to quiz you on what you've been discussing. Just say something like *"Quiz me on what we just covered"* or *"Give me five questions on this topic."*

<Tip>
  This is a great way to consolidate a study session. After talking through a subject with Clamly Chat, switching into quiz mode immediately tests whether you've actually absorbed it — not just whether you followed along.
</Tip>

## Your chat history

All of your Clamly Chat conversations are saved to your account automatically. You can revisit previous sessions from the chat history panel — useful for picking up where you left off or reviewing an explanation you found helpful.

<Note>
  Chat history is tied to your account, so your conversations are available across devices as long as you're logged in.
</Note>

## AI accuracy

Clamly Chat is powered by **Mistral AI** and is designed to be a helpful study companion — but it can make mistakes.

<Warning>
  AI responses may contain inaccuracies or incomplete information. Never rely solely on Clamly Chat for academic work. Always verify important facts, formulas, and explanations against your course materials or authoritative sources.
</Warning>

Think of Clamly Chat as a knowledgeable study partner, not a substitute for your textbooks or instructors.
