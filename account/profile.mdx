Your Profile page is the control centre for everything personal to your Clamly account. From here you can update your display name, switch themes, set your study purpose, and manage privacy preferences — all without leaving a single screen.

## Updating Your Profile Information

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Profile">
    Click your avatar or initials in the top-right corner of any page, then select **Profile** from the dropdown menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Your Details">
    Click the **Edit** button next to the field you want to change. You can update:
    - **Name / Display name** — the name shown across the app and on your achievements
    - **Email address** — used for login and any account notifications
    - **Country** — helps Clamly surface relevant exam boards and curricula
    - **Study purpose** — e.g. school exams, university, professional certification
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **Save** after editing each field. Changes take effect immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If you change your email address, Clamly will send a confirmation link to the new address. Your old email remains active until you verify the new one.
</Note>

## Choosing Your App Theme

Clamly supports light and dark themes so you can study comfortably in any environment.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Profile">
    Navigate to your Profile page as described above.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a Theme">
    Under **Appearance**, choose **Light**, **Dark**, or **System** (follows your device's setting). The preview updates instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Cookie and Analytics Preferences

Clamly uses analytics only to improve the product. You can withdraw or update your consent at any time.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Cookie Preferences">
    Scroll to the **Privacy** section on your Profile page and click **Manage Cookie Preferences**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Toggle Consent">
    Enable or disable analytics cookies using the toggles provided, then click **Save Preferences**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Withdrawing analytics consent does not affect your ability to use Clamly. Core functionality cookies are always required.
</Tip>

## Exporting Your Data

You can download a copy of everything Clamly holds about you — including your quizzes, notes, flashcard decks, and more — directly from your Profile page. See the [Data & Privacy](/account/data-export) guide for full details on what the export contains and your GDPR rights.

## Deleting Your Account

<Warning>
  Account deletion is **permanent and irreversible**. All your data — including quizzes, flashcard decks, notes, todos, exam entries, AI chat history, uploaded files, and achievements — will be removed within 30 days. There is no way to recover your account after deletion.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Profile">
    Open your Profile page from the top-right menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scroll to Danger Zone">
    Scroll to the bottom of the page to find the **Delete Account** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    Click **Delete Account**, then confirm your decision in the dialogue that appears. You may be asked to re-enter your password or confirm via email.
  </Step>
</Steps>
