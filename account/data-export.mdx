Your data belongs to you. You can download a full copy of everything stored in your account at any time, request corrections, withdraw consent, or permanently close your account.

## Exporting your data

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Your Profile Page">
    Click your avatar or initials in the top-right corner of any Clamly page, then select **Profile**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Export Data">
    Scroll to the **Data & Privacy** section and click the **Export Data** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for the Export to Prepare">
    Clamly will compile your data. Depending on how much content you have, this may take a few moments.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download Your File">
    Once ready, a download will begin automatically (or a download link will appear). Save the file to your device.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your export file is generated on demand and is only available to you. Clamly does not store or share the generated export file.
</Note>

## Your GDPR rights

Clamly is operated from Romania and is subject to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). If you are located in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or another jurisdiction with equivalent data protection law, you have the following rights over your personal data:

<Accordion title="Right of Access">
  You have the right to receive a copy of all personal data Clamly holds about you. Use the **Export Data** button on your Profile page to exercise this right instantly.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Right to Correction">
  You have the right to request that inaccurate or incomplete personal data be corrected. You can update most information directly on your Profile page. For anything you can't edit yourself, email [support@clamly.app](mailto:support@clamly.app).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Right to Deletion (Right to be Forgotten)">
  You have the right to request that Clamly delete your personal data. You can initiate account deletion directly from your Profile page, or submit a request to [support@clamly.app](mailto:support@clamly.app). All personal data is removed within **30 days** of a confirmed deletion request.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Right to Data Portability">
  You have the right to receive your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format. The **Export Data** button on your Profile page fulfils this right.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Right to Withdraw Consent">
  Where Clamly processes your data based on your consent (such as analytics cookies), you can withdraw that consent at any time from the **Privacy** section of your Profile page. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing that occurred before you withdrew consent.
</Accordion>

## Requesting corrections or deletion by email

<Steps>
  <Step title="Email Support">
    Send your request to [support@clamly.app](mailto:support@clamly.app) from the email address associated with your Clamly account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe Your Request">
    State what you need: correction of a specific data field, or full account and data deletion.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Await Confirmation">
    The Clamly team will acknowledge your request and action it in accordance with applicable law. Account and data deletions are completed within **30 days**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Deleting your account permanently removes all associated data: quizzes, flashcard decks, notes, todos, exam entries, AI chat history, uploaded files, and achievements. This action cannot be undone. Export your data before requesting deletion if you want to keep a copy.
</Warning>
