Everything you create in Clamly belongs to you — and sharing it with others should be just as simple as creating it. Whether you want to distribute a quiz to your entire study group or export your notes for offline study, Clamly gives you straightforward sharing options that keep you in control of your content at every step.

## Sharing quizzes

The primary way to share a quiz in Clamly is by posting it to a study group. Once shared, every current group member will have immediate access to it from the group's shared content feed — and any member who joins later will see it there too.

### How to share a quiz

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your quiz">
    Go to the **Quizzes** section and open the quiz you want to share.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Share">
    Select the **Share** button in the top-right corner of the quiz page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a group">
    Select **Share with Group** and pick the study group you want to share it with from your list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Confirm your choice. The quiz is now visible to all members of that group.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## You always own your content

<Note>
  No matter how widely you share a quiz or set of notes, you retain full ownership of everything you create in Clamly. Sharing gives others the ability to view and use your content — it does not transfer any rights to them.
</Note>

Clamly's approach is simple: your content is yours. You decide who sees it, and you can stop sharing it at any time by removing it from a group.

## Exporting notes

If you need your notes outside of Clamly — for offline study, printing, or sharing through another channel — you can export them directly from the Notes page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Notes">
    Open the **Notes** section from the main navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a note">
    Open the note you want to export.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export">
    Click the **Export** button and choose your preferred format. Your note will download to your device.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Exporting is a great way to create a personal backup of your notes or share them with study partners who aren't on Clamly yet.
</Tip>

## What happens to shared content when you leave a group

When you leave a study group, any quizzes or content you previously shared with that group remain visible to its members — your material doesn't disappear just because you've left. If you want to remove specific content from the group before leaving, delete or unshare it first.

<Accordion title="How to remove a quiz from a group before leaving">
  Open the quiz from the group's shared content feed, click **Manage Sharing**, and select **Remove from Group**. The quiz will no longer be visible to group members, and you'll still have it in your personal Quizzes library.
</Accordion>

## You control what you share

Clamly is designed around your privacy. Nothing is shared without a deliberate action from you — there are no public feeds or automatic sharing of your quizzes and notes. Every share is initiated by you, and every share can be undone.

<Note>
  Review what you've shared periodically from the **Sharing** settings in your profile. You can see all active group shares and revoke any of them with one click.
</Note>
