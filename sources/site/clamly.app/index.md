## Common questions

What is Clamly?

Clamly is an all-in-one study platform for students. It combines AI-generated quizzes, an AI study assistant, spaced-repetition flashcards, notes, a focus timer, study groups, and exam scheduling in a single web app.

Is Clamly free?

Yes. All core features are free with session-based AI usage. Starter ($4.99/mo) and Pro ($9.99/mo) plans unlock more AI sessions and extra features.

What can I upload to Clamly AI?

PDFs, Word docs, plain text, or pasted notes. Clamly AI reads them and generates quizzes plus a summary.

What is Clamly Chat?

An AI study assistant with four modes — Adaptive, Tutor, Buddy, and Professor. It explains concepts, answers questions, and can turn the conversation into a quiz.

Can I study with friends?

Yes. Create a study group, invite friends with an invite code, and share quizzes inside it. Owners and admins manage members and roles.

Does the Pomodoro timer follow me across pages?

Yes — it runs globally. A mini timer stays pinned to the app so you never lose track of your session.

What are flashcards?

Clamly uses spaced repetition (SM-2 algorithm) to help you review at the right time. Track due cards, retention rate, and master your decks.
