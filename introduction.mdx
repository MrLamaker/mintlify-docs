**Clamly** is a study workspace for students who want to learn faster and remember more. Upload your notes, generate quizzes with AI, drill flashcards with spaced repetition, run Pomodoro sessions, and collaborate with classmates — all from one app, on any device, with a free plan that doesn't require a credit card.

## How students use Clamly

<Steps>
  <Step title="Bring in your material">
    Upload PDFs, Word docs, or paste your notes. Clamly's AI reads everything and turns it into study-ready content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate quizzes and summaries">
    Hit generate and get instant quizzes (Normal, Hard, or Hell difficulty), key highlights, and a clean summary of any document.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drill with flashcards">
    Build SM-2 spaced-repetition decks so you review the right cards at the right time — and stop wasting time on what you already know.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stay focused, stay on track">
    Run Pomodoro sessions that follow you across the app, track exams on a synced calendar, and check off todos as you go.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Behind Clamly

Clamly was founded by **Robert Sabo**, a student who got tired of bouncing between five different apps to study for one exam. He built Clamly to be the tool he wished he'd had — a single, focused workspace that combines AI, spaced repetition, and collaboration without the bloat. Robert's goal is simple: make effective studying accessible to every student, regardless of background or budget.
