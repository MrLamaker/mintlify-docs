Clamly is an all-in-one study platform built for students who want to study smarter. Whether you're preparing for a big exam, keeping up with daily coursework, or collaborating with classmates, Clamly brings every tool you need into one place — powered by AI and designed to adapt to the way you learn.

## What You Can Do with Clamly

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="AI Quizzes" icon="brain">
    Generate quizzes instantly from your own PDFs and documents. Choose from Normal, Hard, or Hell difficulty to match your preparation level.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Clamly Chat" icon="message-bot">
    Chat with an AI study assistant in four modes — Adaptive, Tutor, Buddy, and Professor — tailored to how you want to learn.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Flashcards" icon="cards-blank">
    Build flashcard decks powered by SM-2 spaced repetition so you review the right cards at exactly the right time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Focus Timer" icon="timer">
    Stay on task with a built-in Pomodoro timer. The global timer keeps running across pages so your flow is never broken.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Notes" icon="notebook">
    Write in Markdown with full-screen mode, then pin, color-code, and export your notes however you like.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Todos" icon="circle-check">
    Manage tasks with priority levels, due dates, and drag-and-drop reordering to keep your study plan on track.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Exam Scheduler" icon="calendar">
    Schedule upcoming exams on a visual calendar and sync them directly to Google Calendar with one click.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Study Groups" icon="users">
    Create or join study groups to collaborate, share resources, and keep each other accountable.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Achievements & Streaks" icon="trophy">
    Earn achievements and maintain daily streaks to stay motivated and track your long-term consistency.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Who Clamly Is For

Clamly is designed for students at any level — high school, university, or self-study. If you upload notes, sit exams, or want to retain information more effectively, Clamly has a tool that fits your workflow. All core features are available on the free plan with no credit card required.

<Note>
  You must be at least 13 years old to create a Clamly account.
</Note>
