## Who Clamly is for

Clamly is built for students at any level — high school, university, or self-study. If you upload notes, sit exams, or simply want to remember more of what you study, there's a tool here for your workflow. Every core feature is available on the free plan, with no credit card required.

<Note>
  You must be at least 13 years old to create a Clamly account.
</Note>

## About the founder

Clamly was founded by **Robert Sabo**, who built the platform to give students a single, focused place to study smarter. Drawing on his own experience navigating exams and coursework, Robert designed Clamly around the tools he wished he'd had — AI-generated quizzes, spaced-repetition flashcards, distraction-free notes, and study groups, all in one app. His mission is simple: make effective studying accessible to every student, regardless of background or budget.
